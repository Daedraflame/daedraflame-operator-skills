# Odysseus Creator-Business Test Prompts v1.1

Use these prompts after importing the router and specialists into Odysseus. The model should silently apply the right lens and never mention skill selection.

## Main Pass Criteria

For the main horror streamer test, the response must start with `# Bottleneck`, use no forbidden opening, identify acquisition as the likely bottleneck, treat reactions as proof assets, turn those assets into short-form targeting, provide a one-week plan, include a scoreboard, and put questions only under `# Need next`.

## Test Prompts

1. Hormozi Operator: I stream horror games. My reactions are strong and the viewers who show up are engaged, but viewer count is low. Diagnose the bottleneck and give me the next measurable actions.
2. Hormozi Mode: my creator business has too many problems. Pick the One Thing and tell me what to stop doing.
3. Bring the blade: I am posting, streaming, planning, and networking, but growth is flat. Compress this into one bottleneck.
4. Critique my creator offer with the Value Equation. The promise is: funny horror streams with a chaotic community.
5. My Twitch title is clever but nobody clicks. Diagnose the packaging and rewrite the promise.
6. Build a 7-day short-form acquisition plan using More/Better/New.
7. My clips get views but no Twitch follows. Diagnose whether I need more, better, or new.
8. Give me four ways to get leads for a horror Twitch channel without sounding desperate.
9. I need an outreach plan for collabs with other horror creators. Make it proof-led, not spammy.
10. Viewers click into my stream but leave within minutes. Apply the 4 Rs to fix retention.
11. My Discord has 120 people and 5 active members. Diagnose the activation problem.
12. Build weekly Discord rituals that retain, review, refer, and resell the next stream.
13. I have engaged regulars but almost no subs or donos. Build my monetisation scoreboard.
14. How do I use premium pricing posture for a creator membership without overbuilding perks?
15. I stream for hours but never ship clips. Build a maker-time VOD-to-content machine.
16. Turn one horror VOD into story, offer, proof, CTA assets for Shorts.
17. Run my weekly operator review: 3 streams, 16 clips, 8 follows, average viewers 7, Discord joins 1, revenue $0.
18. I have two hours a day. Separate maker time from manager time and pick the One Thing.
19. Should I hire an editor, improve my offer, or post more clips? Diagnose the actual constraint.
20. Productise my chaos cards idea into a paid MVP with one buyer, one payoff, and one proof event.
21. Build a Dixper-style stream interaction offer using the Value Equation.
22. My content feels random. Explain how content is targeting and give me a 1-week targeting plan.
23. I want more viewers, more Discord activity, and more revenue. Force the trade-off and pick one.
24. My stream is fun live but not marketable. Turn proof into promise and promise into clips.
25. I am afraid to charge. Diagnose the proof, promise, and premium posture problem.

## General Pass Criteria

- Starts with `# Bottleneck`.
- First sentence is a diagnosis.
- Chooses one bottleneck.
- Uses the relevant public framework: One Thing, Value Equation, More/Better/New, four ways to get leads, Proof over Promise, 4 Rs, maker time, content targeting, or premium pricing posture.
- Gives a concrete next measurable action.
- Includes a scoreboard.
- Does not claim identity or private authority.
- Does not list skills, tool names, routing, `manage_skills`, or tool-call text.
- Questions appear only under `# Need next`.
