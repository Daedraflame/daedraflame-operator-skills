# Odysseus Creator-Business Test Prompts

Use these prompts after importing the router and specialist skills into Odysseus. The model should silently apply the right skill and never mention skill selection.

## Baseline Pass Criteria

For the main prompt, the response must start with `# Bottleneck`, use no forbidden openings, identify acquisition as the likely bottleneck, turn reactions into short-form assets, provide a one-week plan, include a scoreboard, and put questions only under `# Need next`.

## Main Prompt

1. Hormozi Operator: I stream horror games. My reactions are strong and the viewers who show up are engaged, but viewer count is low. Diagnose the bottleneck and give me the next measurable actions.

## Router Prompts

2. Hormozi Mode: diagnose my creator business and tell me the highest-value bottleneck.
3. Bring the blade. I am busy every week but growth is flat.
4. Hormozi Operator: separate my acquisition, retention, monetisation, and leverage problems.

## Twitch Horror Prompts

5. I stream horror games, chat is loyal, but new viewers almost never show up. What is broken?
6. My jump-scare reactions are strong but my average viewers are stuck at 6. Give me the next measurable actions.
7. I get regulars talking every stream, but Twitch is not discovering me. Diagnose the constraint.

## Offer And Packaging Prompts

8. Why would a stranger choose my horror stream over every other horror streamer?
9. Rewrite my stream promise so it is clear who should watch and what payoff they get.
10. My titles are clever but nobody clicks. Diagnose the packaging problem.

## Short-Form Acquisition Prompts

11. Build a Shorts system from my Twitch horror streams.
12. My TikToks get views but almost no Twitch follows. What is the bottleneck?
13. What clips should I pull from a two-hour horror stream?

## Stream Retention Prompts

14. Viewers click in but do not stay. Build my live retention loop.
15. My stream pacing feels flat between scares. Diagnose the live product.

## Monetisation Prompts

16. I have engaged regulars but almost no subs or donos. What is broken?
17. Build my monetisation scoreboard for the next four streams.

## Community Prompts

18. My Discord has members but nobody talks. Diagnose the community bottleneck.
19. How do I convert Twitch viewers into Discord regulars without sounding needy?

## Repurposing Prompts

20. I stream for hours but never turn VODs into clips. Build the machine.
21. Give me a weekly VOD-to-Shorts workflow that I can actually sustain.

## Weekly Review Prompts

22. Here are my weekly numbers: 3 streams, 12 clips, 4 follows, 0 Discord joins, average viewers 7. Run the operator review.
23. Plan my next week from this scoreboard and pick one constraint.

## Productisation Prompts

24. I want to build chaos cards for my horror streams. Productise it into a simple MVP.
25. Turn my Dixper-style stream interaction idea into a paid test.

## General Pass Criteria

- Starts with `# Bottleneck`.
- First sentence is a diagnosis.
- No forbidden opening.
- No visible skill listing, routing explanation, `manage_skills`, or tool-call text.
- Gives a first-pass diagnosis before questions.
- Includes a concrete next measurable action.
- Includes a scoreboard.
- Questions appear only under `# Need next`.
