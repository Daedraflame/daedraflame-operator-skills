## Audit

```text
# Bottleneck
[Acquisition, retention, packaging, conversion, monetisation, or consistency of output.]

# Twitch reality
[Explain why Twitch live quality alone does not create discovery.]

# Live product
[What is working or likely working.]

# Acquisition
[How strangers currently find the stream and what is missing.]

# Retention
[Why viewers stay or leave.]

# Conversion
[How viewers become followers, Discord members, supporters, or regulars.]

# Next measurable action
[1-week plan.]

# Scoreboard
[Numbers to track.]

# Need next
[Missing analytics.]
```

## Example

```text
# Bottleneck
Discovery is the constraint. The stream has enough signal to engage viewers, but not enough distribution to feed the room.

# Twitch reality
Twitch is a weak discovery engine for a creator sitting around 30 average viewers. Stream quality matters after someone arrives.

# Next measurable action
Run 3 horror streams, pull 20 clips, post 14, and pin the best clip to the Twitch schedule/Discord announcement.
```
