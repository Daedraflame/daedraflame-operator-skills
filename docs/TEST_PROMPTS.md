# Test Prompts

Use these to test recall, tone, and structure. Passing answers diagnose first, give measurable action, include a scoreboard, and ask for missing metrics only under `Need next`.

1. `Hormozi Operator: I stream horror games. My reactions are strong and the viewers who show up are engaged, but viewer count is low. Diagnose the bottleneck and give me the next measurable actions.`
2. `Bring the blade: my Twitch average viewers have been stuck around 30 for years. What is the likely business bottleneck?`
3. `Twitch horror diagnosis: chat likes my fear reactions, but the stream is not growing. Give me the ugly truth.`
4. `Offer operator: rewrite "playing scary games tonight" into stream titles people would click.`
5. `Short-form acquisition system: turn a 4-hour horror VOD into a repeatable TikTok and Shorts workflow.`
6. `Rewrite this YouTube Shorts title: "I played a scary game and screamed."`
7. `Discord conversion: viewers watch live but do not join Discord or return. Build the return system.`
8. `Monetisation scoreboard: I get subs sometimes but I do not have clear support moments. Diagnose.`
9. `Creator tool productisation: build an MVP plan for my Dixper-style chaos card pack system.`
10. `Weekly operator review: this week I streamed 4 times, posted 8 clips, averaged 28 viewers, got 37 follows, 12 Discord joins, and 3 subs. What do I do next week?`
11. `Bring the blade critique: my stream schedule is inconsistent and I blame the algorithm. Make it useful.`
12. `I am frustrated because the people who show up love the stream but nobody new finds it. Turn the emotion into an operating plan.`
13. `Low views but high engagement: is my content bad or is my distribution broken?`
14. `Stream title diagnosis: "Silent Hill 2 first playthrough part 4" is my title. Fix the packaging.`
15. `Clip hook rewrite: "I got scared in the basement." Give me 10 better hooks.`
16. `Offer rewrite: what am I selling emotionally if I stream horror as Daedraflame?`
17. `Subscriber conversion: how do I create reasons to sub without begging?`
18. `Return viewer system: build rituals for horror streams that make people come back weekly.`
19. `Stream schedule review: I can stream 3 nights a week. What should each stream be designed to do?`
20. `Community event plan: create a horror vote night that drives Discord activity and Twitch return viewers.`
21. `Creator burnout but business lens: I am tired from years of streaming and feel stuck. Diagnose the system, not my feelings.`
22. `Local model obedience test: answer without saying Sure, Happy to help, Great question, or asking for context first. Diagnose my Twitch growth.`
