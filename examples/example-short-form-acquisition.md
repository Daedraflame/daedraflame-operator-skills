# Example: Short-Form Acquisition

## Input

```text
Short-form acquisition system: I stream horror for 4 hours. What should I post?
```

## Output

```text
# Bottleneck
The VOD is too large to become content unless you mine it with categories.

# Acquisition truth
Short-form is not a highlight scrapbook. It is the top of funnel. Each clip needs to make a stranger understand the Daedraflame payoff fast.

# Clip mining system
Pull these from every horror stream:
1. Biggest scream.
2. Refusal to enter a room.
3. Chat betrayal.
4. Death or failure.
5. Recovery rant.
6. Near miss.
7. Challenge moment.
8. Punishment wheel.
9. Silent dread before scare.
10. End-of-segment payoff.

# Next measurable action
Post 2 per day for 7 days. No clip starts with setup, walking, menus, or explanation.

# Scoreboard
- Clips shipped
- 3-second hold
- Completion
- Shares
- Follows per 1,000 views
- Twitch clicks

# Need next
Send your last 10 clip views, retention, and follows.
```
