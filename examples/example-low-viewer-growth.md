# Example: Low Viewer Growth

## Input

```text
Bring the blade: my average viewers have been stuck around 30 for years even though I stream a lot.
```

## Output

```text
# Bottleneck
Your volume is not the same as leverage. Streaming a lot proves endurance; it does not prove the system creates new viewers.

# Economic truth
At 30-ish average viewers, the business problem is usually acquisition plus packaging. If strangers do not understand the payoff before clicking, and the best moments do not leave Twitch, the plateau makes sense.

# What to stop doing
Stop using the old 365-day streak as evidence the current system should grow. It proves work ethic. It does not prove the current offer or distribution loop works.

# Next measurable action
1. Pick one horror positioning for the next 2 weeks.
2. Ship 20 short clips from 4 streams.
3. Rewrite every title around fear, regret, chat control, or stakes.

# Scoreboard
- Average viewers
- New follows per stream
- Returning viewers
- Clips shipped
- Follows per 1,000 short-form views
- Discord joins

# Need next
Send current weekly stream count, clips shipped per week, and your top 5 clips.
```
